INTRODUCTION: Biometric techniques are very much useful in this
digital era especially in institutions and organizations for maintaining
the attendance system simply and most easily. The expertise lies in the
technique are being adopted for the attendance system. Amongst
several biometric techniques in use, face recognition has its advantages.
In this paper, for object detection, Haar cascade classifier is used and
trained for a set of positive and negative images.For improvising the
quality of teaching-learning process and to reduce the dropping of
classes by students, this automatic attendance management system will
be very much useful. In the conventional attendance system, each
student will be called and wait for their response to mark the time-
consuming attendance. Also, the students will come at the end of taking
attendance and again their presence has to be registered. To reduce
these kinds of confusing and manual interfering situations, this method
of machine learning-based attendance management system finds very
useful.
ABSTRACT: Face recognition technology has made many developments
in the world. Attendance using face recognition is a real-time solution
which involves handling of a person’s attendance system. Face
recognition-based attendance system will be able to find and recognize
human faces fast and precise in images or videos that are being
captured through a camera. The traditional method of marking
attendance is time-consuming and maintenance is difficult. There were
so many methods like eigen faces, fisher faces etc. In this paper, the
performance of face detection is implemented by a machine learning
cascade classifier technique. It recognizes the faces in the image and
compares it with the trained faces in the database. On identification of a
registered face on the acquired image collections, the attendance of a
person is registered automatically without manual interruption.
APPROACH: For using the proposed system there must be an
administrator. For example, when using this system in a class, the class
counselor may be the administrator who is responsible for the entire
process till the completion. The access into the system is possible only if
the administrator sign-in to the system. For users, login is necessary and
hence they must be registered initially. Registration process involves
collection of basic details of the users. Based on the settings, ‘n’ number
of sample images of the users are captured as one of the steps in
registration process. In this work, 100 images of the user iS captured.
Detecting facial parameters such as eyes, eyebrows, nose, mouth, and
jawline is one of the shape prediction problems. Facial features are used
to confine and symbolize prominent regions of the face. A shape
predictor confines the facial features and the shape, once the user’s
image is fed into the system. Shape prediction methods are used to
detect facial landmarks. Hence it involves confining the face in the
image and sensing the basic features on the face ROI. A model and built-
in Haar classifier are used for prediction of facial features and hence
face recognition is realized by above two ways. The facial images caught
by the camera is processed and converted to grayscale.
PRIMARY GOAL: The primary objective of the following system is to
capture the face of each person and to store it in the database for their
attendance.
There is no need to manually take attendance in the organization
because the system captures a photo and through further departing
steps the face is being recognized and the attendance record is updated
APPLICATION: Being a prime feature of biometric verification,
facial recognition is being used enormously in several such
applications, like video monitoring and CCTV footage
